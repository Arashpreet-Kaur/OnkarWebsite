Description and history of Heritage Site 1.



The Golden Temple, also known as Sri Harmandir Sahib, is one of the most revered spiritual sites in Sikhism. Located in the city of Amritsar, in the Indian state of Punjab, it is a symbol of human brotherhood and equality, embodying the Sikh principles of humility and service.


Langar:One of the most important practices at the Golden Temple is the Langar, a community kitchen that serves free meals to all visitors, regardless of caste, creed, or religion. This tradition underscores the Sikh principles of service, equality, and community.
